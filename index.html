How to play

Classic Mode

BombFlip is a game in wich you flip over cards to find numbers hidden beneath them.

The cards are hiding the numbers 1 through 3... and bombs as well.

The first number you flip over will give you that many points.

From them on, the number you find will add to the total amount of points you've collected by that number.

If it's a 2, your total will be increased by 2.

If it's a 3, your total will be increased by 3.

But if you flip over a 1, your total will stay the same. And if you flip over a bomb, it's game over.

When that happens, you'll lose all the points you've collected in the corrent game.

Once you've found all the hidden 2 and 3 cards, you've cleared the game and will advance to the next level of difficulty.

As the difficulty increases, you will be able to receive more points. Do your best!


The numbers at the side of the board will give you a clue about the numbers hidden on the back of the panels.

The larger the number, the more likely it is that there are many large numbers hidden in that row or column.

In the same way, you can tell how many bombs are hidden in the row or column.

Consider the hidden number totals and the bombs totals carefully as you flip over panels.

Timer Mode

In this mode you will have a timer that will show how much time you have left before the end of the game.

The game starts with 90 seconds, you can increase the time by flipping cards.

The time will increase based on the number of the card flipped.

If it's a 1, your total time will be increased by 1 second.

If it's a 2, it will be increased by 2 seconds.

And for a 3, 3 seconds.

If you flip a bomb, game over. When that happens, you'll go back at the starting point.

Once you've found all the hidden 2 and 3 cards, you've cleared the game and will advance to the next level of difficulty.

The timer will maintain the time left through the difficulty levels, so be quick!
